PROJECT VISION AND DESCRIPTION

Insert your Vision Statement here an d a brief description of your vision that includes the name of the organization or issue, location and mission of the organization or an explanation of the cause. Include your vision of the need you are addressing and how your plan to meet this need will help those you are serving.

Include an explanation of why this is important to you, an explanation of what you will offer and how (include group members you plan to work with) you will address this need.

A vision statement is a vivid idealized description of a desired outcome that inspires, energizes and helps you create a mental picture of your target. It could be a vision of a part of your life, for the group or community or the ultimate outcome of a project or goal.

Examples of vision statements:

  • "No child in our city will go hungry to bed in the evening." (Soup kitchen)
  • "In two decades our services will no longer be needed." (Illiteracy program)
  • "We will be recognized as the best symphony orchestra in America." (Symphony orchestra)

DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITIES/ INVOLVEMENT (In details describe the activities, your involvement in the activities, who was involved/participated, etc.) 

Over lunch, I met with Meekyung Kim, former high school teacher and Gyuho Kim a professor at Korean Air Force Academy. First I explained to them the purpose and the nature of senior service project. After, I explained the specifics of my project individualized to fit my goal an objective, Mrs. Kim suggested that I may focus on the eye-level of the elementary students if I intend to stay with the elementary setting. So I noted to myself that I should change the objectives to fit the age level of the participants of this project. 

Next I told them how I was to accomplish such goals: through summer enrichment program. Then they both questioned how I was to get the program accepted by the school and if I had a way of knowing the interest level among the students. It dawned on me then that I was clueless as to exactly how I was to implement this program. Not only that, I realized that I was totally naïve about the interest level of students a rural school

Mr. Kim enlightened me with the way the Air Force Academy implements English summer classes and volunteer work for cadets as well as children. The Program for children first takes volunteers and from them the specifics of the programs are formulated and carried out. So he implied that maybe the elementary school had a similar system where a certain camp or a program could the main possible involvement of the volunteers. So I decided to go visit the school myself in order to have a better grasp of what opportunities are available.

ACCOMPLISMENT (List the results, products, or outputs of the day)

1. modified objectives

2. solid and specific steps towards project achievement: visit possible volunteer site

3. another perspective of how I can accomplish my goal-through an already organized program

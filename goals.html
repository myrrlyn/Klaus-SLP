Goals, Objectives, and Activities

This is a review of the goal-setting process.

Goal: A concise statement of a desired purpose toward achievement.
Objectives: Steps taken that lead to goal accomplishment.
Activities: Actions taken that lead to goal achievement. They typically have a defined time period

  • Goal: Provide excellence in service to Mackinac Island
  • Measurable Outcome:
    1. Tourists are satisfied
    2. Tasks are completed
    3. Island is improved
  • Objectives:
    1. Provide excellence in show:
      • marching form
      • uniform quality
      • timeliness
    2. Provide excellence in service:
      • Duty shifts in fort and downtown
      • Off-duty service projects such as cleaning and garbage collection
      • Flag retirement
  • Activities:
    1. Drill Competition
    2. Flag Presentation and Retreat
    3. Off-duty activities (going downtown, touring the island)
